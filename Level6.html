Level 6 Dissertation

How has psychoanalysis influenced the visual art since the 19th century?

Psychoanalysis, Surrealism and Art: A Comparative Study.

The Visual Subconscious: How art accesses parts of the brain we cannot.



For my dissertation I would like to focus on the psyche and art particularly investigating how the psyche is explored, accessed and represented through visual communication and the impact psychoanalysis has had on creating art and aesthetics, exploring the surrealist movements and art techniques that aid in accesses the subconscious whilst looking at André Masson, André Breton Salvador Dali, Max Ernst, Sir Roland Penrose and others.


Research sources:

- Surrealism by Patrick Waldberg

- Psychoanalysis and art: Dialogues in the creative process by Eva D. Papiasvili and Linda A. Mayers
